### FORMATTING GUIDELINES

**Length:** Position papers should typically be 1-2 pages (single-spaced) or 2-4 pages (double-spaced)

**Font:** Times New Roman, 12pt

**Margins:** 1 inch on all sides

**Citation Style:** MLA, APA, or Chicago (check your conference's requirements)

**Submission:** Follow your conference's submission guidelines and deadlines

---

**Tips for Success:**
- Start with thorough research about your country and the topic
- Use credible sources (UN documents, government websites, academic journals)
- Be realistic - represent your country's actual position, not your personal views
- Cite all sources properly
- Proofread carefully for grammar and spelling
- Submit on time!
